About Jaden

Jaden loves writing introspective essays and filming videos. His works can be eloquent and poignant, but in reality, Jaden is playful and comedic, often noisy and weird in the most awkward situations. Jaden plans to travel the world in a few years and write a book about his experiences. He also makes YouTube videos regularly, talking about life, philosophy, media, and more, while playing games.



Long Season: A long and winding journey into life

Long Season is an introspective journey through the life of Euri Jaden Orogo. In this deeply personal book, Jaden shares the challenges, triumphs, and profound lessons he's learned along the way. Written with raw honesty and emotional depth, Long Season offers readers a chance to reflect on their own journeys.
